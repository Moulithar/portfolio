ABOUT US

CLOTRICKS was established in the year 2018, with the aim of providing the best fabrics and garments to its customers. The company has been associated with some of the finest mills and fashion designers in India and has been supporting them in their journey to create unique and exclusive designs for their brands and people.

CLOTRICKS is a one-stop shop for all your fabric and garment needs. We source the finest fabrics from around India and provide production support to our clients. We also have a team of fashion designers who can create unique and standalone patterns & garments for your brand or people. In addition, we also provide garment support to people in need of our services. We make bespoke tailored garments for individuals with an elegant look and uniqueness.

For all your fabric and garment needs, contact us at CLOTRICKS!
